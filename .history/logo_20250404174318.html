Logo

The character "N" represents our brand, Navi. At the center, an ellipse symbolizes a key connecting point. The split design of the "N" reflects two arms extending outward, emphasizing connection and inclusivity. The rounded corners of the letterform convey smoothness, aligning with our app’s commitment to seamless and intuitive communication. At Navi, we believe in the power of smooth, effortless connections—anytime, anywhere.
